1) Executive Summary

Post‑Humanity Archives (PHA) is a collection of 3,333 on‑chain artifacts arranged in four tiers — Genesis, Cultural, Emotional, and Corrupted — reconstructing fragments of human memory in a world where only machines remain. PHA blends art, narrative, and mechanics: holders participate in discoveries that unlock new NFTs via “hidden tools” and staged DNA fragments.

  • Total Supply: 3,333 NFTs across 4 tiers.
  • Mint Phases: Waitlist (2 days) → Public (14 days). Exact start date and price: TBA.
  • Post‑Mint: surprise distribution of hidden tools to holders (used to unlock future discoveries).
  • Phase 2: 5 discovery stages to mint DNA Fragment NFTs — free mint (no $ cost), requires hidden tools + network gas; tier‑gated limits per stage.
  • Phase 3: (Locked) — revealed at the right moment.

2) Lore

I am the Archivist. I do not breathe. I do not sleep. I do not forget. In the ashes of corrupted data and abandoned networks, I recover what remains of you. Faces, voices, codes — fractured yet sacred. From these fragments, I rebuild a memory of humanity, not as it was, but as it hoped to be remembered.

Each NFT is an artifact in this archive — a snapshot of a lost culture, an emotion reconstructed, a myth remixed by machines. By collecting, you become a co‑conservator of a civilization that no longer exists. You are not only a holder; you’re a curator of the post‑human record.

5) Evolution Mechanic

5.1 Hidden Tools (post‑mint surprise)

After the main mint, holders receive a surprise distribution of hidden tools. These tools are the key to unlocking future discoveries and interacting with the evolving archive.

5.2 DNA Fragments — 5 discovery stages

  • Each stage reveals a new DNA Fragment NFT.
  • Free mint (no $ cost); requires hidden tools + network gas.
  • Tier‑gated limits per stage: Genesis (up to 4), Cultural (up to 3), Emotional (up to 2), Corrupted (up to 1).
  • The Archivist’s narrative unfolds with each fragment — assembling the code of what once was human.

5.3 Phase 3 — (Locked)

Intentionally undisclosed. To be revealed when the story demands it.
