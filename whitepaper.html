Abstract

Post-Humanity Archives (PHA) is a collection of 3,333 digital artifacts — reconstructed fragments of memory from a post-human civilization. Each piece is crafted in glitch art + vaporwave + neural dystopia aesthetics, designed as PFP-ready NFTs. Beyond their artistic identity, these artifacts embody a narrative of machines recovering the traces of humanity, blending lore, culture, and technology.

Vision

The project envisions an evolving archive — not static collectibles, but living fragments that connect holders to future phases of discovery. Each NFT represents a memory, corrupted yet beautiful, waiting to be pieced together in the collective reconstruction of what humanity once was.

Collection Structure

  • 3,333 total NFTs
  • 4 tiers:
    • Genesis (100)
    • Cultural (555)
    • Emotional (1000)
    • Corrupted (1678)

Each tier reflects different levels of recovery, corruption, and rarity.

Resource Allocation (Initial Sale)

  • 20% — Project creator.
  • 80% — Development of subsequent phases, including:
  • Marketing and Partnerships Fund to expand the community and collaborate with other projects and artists.
  • Creation and deployment of upcoming phases.
  • Team and Operations to sustain project development and infrastructure.
  • Treasury Reserve for future acquisitions and long-term growth.

Secondary Sale Royalties

  • 2% fee applied to every secondary market transaction.

Royalties Distribution

  • 1% — Continuous development fund and platform maintenance.
  • 1% — Reward to the lead artist and collaborators.

Lore & Narrative

PHA is set in a timeline where humanity has vanished, leaving only fragments of digital memories. Machines, now archivists, reconstruct these memories imperfectly, revealing faces, voices, emotions, and corrupted codes. Each NFT is both artifact and story — a shard of a civilization remembered through glitches.

Attributes

Each artifact includes core traits:

  • Archive Origin
  • Corruption Type
  • Emotion Signature
  • Visual Integrity
  • Recovery Level
  • Legacy Seal

Conclusion

Post-Humanity Archives is more than an NFT collection — it is a living memory system, evolving with its community. By holding an artifact, you participate in rebuilding the fragments of humanity, influencing lore, future discoveries, and cultural footprint on-chain.
